Welcome to
Arachne Web Development!

With Arachne Web Development you will be able to create your own online portfolio for your art work. This site will provide the resources and templates that will allow you to market your personal brand and share your work with the world. Portfolio examples are listed below, but we invite you to browse through our Instructions pages so that you can begin to “Weave Your Identity.”

Carousel Example

The Carousel Layout will be heavy in visual content. It will be promoted as the ideal layout options for visual artists like drawers, painters, and photographers. However, anyone can use the template, really.

Grid Example

The Grid Layout will be heavy in textual content. It will be promoted as the ideal layout options for artists that work primarily with text or sound, like writers and musicians. However, anyone can use the template, really.
